Week Four of Classes

My log post about the fourth week of the 2022 school year.

October 5, 2022 Ian Wood

This week I started the advanced project, which required me to develop an original web application that allows people to add pages to a pre-existing story. We started by generating ideas and outlining our particular plan of action. We made the decision to use a machine learning model to proceduraly generate stories, which would then be updated or expanded upon by certified authors. The landing page was then where we started. To make the project considerably simpler to program in terms of html, we decided to use react.js and tailwind css.

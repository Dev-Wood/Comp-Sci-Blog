Week Eight of Classes

My blog post about the eighth week of the 2022 school year.

November 2, 2022 Ian Wood

Woefully, our valiant efforts this week did not yield the desired outcome. We toiled to create models for our story generator, using the works of the bard himself as our guide. But alas, the tales generated were not fit for young audiences, and so our initial training on Shakespeare's plays proved fruitless. Still, we press on, ever in search of the perfect narrative for all to enjoy.

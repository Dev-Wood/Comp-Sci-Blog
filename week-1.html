Week One of Classes

My blog post about the first week of the 2022 school year.

September 12, 2022 Ian Wood

This past week was the first week of classes for me and it was great. I loved getting to hang out with all of my friends again, and meet my new teachers. I also loved getting back into the swing of things with my schoolwork. It felt great to be back in the classroom and learning new things again. Overall, I had a great first week of classes and I am looking forward to the rest of the semester.

Week Seven of Classes

My blog post about the seventh week of the 2022 school year.

October 25, 2022 Ian Wood

This week, the systems for verifying the person who is currently logged in have been finished. Our team also worked on the menu for AI story generation. With the use of artificial intelligence, users will be able to create and post original stories based on a prompt given to the AI. To make sure that everyone was on the same page, we cleared up some ambiguities we had. This made sure we weren't running into any misunderstandings down the line. Overall the team made great strides towards getting to a state where we can start on the bulk of the project.
